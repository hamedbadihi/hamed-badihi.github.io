Open Positions

Postdoctoral Researcher:

We have a Postdoctoral Researcher position in “Condition Monitoring focusing on Digitalization and Artificial Intelligence (AI) for Critical Energy Infrastructures.” This offers an exciting opportunity to engage in innovative research at the intersection of digital technologies, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and energy system resilience, contributing to the development of smarter, safer and more secure energy infrastructures that are essential for the sustainable future.

For additional details and to submit your application for this position, please follow the link to the job announcement [link].

If you have any questions, please contact Prof. Hamed Badihi (hamed.badihi@tuni.fi).

Application Deadline: Closed!

Doctoral Researcher:

We have a Doctoral Researcher position in “Automatic Control for Resilient Cyber-Physical Renewable Energy and Power Systems.” This represents an exciting opportunity to engage in innovative research at the intersection of advanced control systems, cyber-physical systems, and renewable energies (wind, solar, microgrids, etc.)

The position will be filled for a fixed-term period of four (4) years, commencing in October 2024 or as soon after that as possible. A typical starting salary for a Doctoral Researcher is approximately 2700 EUR per month (plus an annual vacation bonus). The salary increases based on experience and the progress of doctoral studies.

To apply for this position, please contact Prof. Hamed Badihi (hamed.badihi@tuni.fi). Along with your email, attach your CV, transcripts, publications (if any), and English language proficiency information.

Application Deadline: Open until an ideal candidate is selected.

Welcome Applications from Outstanding Applicants:

In addition to the current open position(s), we are continually looking for highly motivated Postdoctoral Researchers, Ph.D., and M.Sc. students with strong backgrounds in areas like Artificial Intelligence, Cybernetics, Systems, and Control.

Funding Opportunities:

Qualified candidates may receive full funding through salaried positions and scholarships. At Tampere University (TAU), it is possible to undertake your entire study program in English.

If you are interested in joining the DARES group, please contact Prof. Hamed Badihi (hamed.badihi@tuni.fi).

Along with your email, attach your CV, transcripts (for Ph.D. and M.Sc. applicants), selected publications (if any), and English language proficiency information. Additionally, include contact information for your current advisor and at least one other reference (name, affiliation, and email address). Note that references will only be contacted after your selection in the application process and with your notification.
